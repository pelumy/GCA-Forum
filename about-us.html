About Girlcode Academy Forum

The Girlcode Academy (GCA) forum is an avenue for potential students, current students, past students and instructors of Girlcode Academy to interact. It is a hub for young programmers to share ideas, opportunities and problens with their mates and more experienced developers. The GirlCode academy platform is focused on human capital development of talented and aspiring young female Nigerians who have passion for acquiring technical skills in areas of software and web application develoment. The project is positioned to encourage the participation of aspiring young women who are willing to delve into the male dominated field of programming and application development
