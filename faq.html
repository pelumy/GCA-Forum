FAQs

Below, you will find answers to the questions we get asked the most about applying into the Girlcode Academy programme.
What is the GirlCode Academy about?

The GirlCode Academy is a non-profit organization empowering young women to obtain skills in STEM; thereby, encouraging them to pursue a career in STEM and bridging the gender gap in the technology industry. You can read more about us and support us by donating.

How do I apply?

The application form will be displayed online when next the academy is accepting a new batch of cohorts. Check our social media platforms and the website regularly, or subscribe to our newsletter to be updated.

What are the entry requirements?

All you need is a working laptop and a learning mindset. You don't need any coding knowledge but having basic knowledge before hand can be of great help to you.

What laptop specification do i need?

You don't need any mind blowing laptop specification to learn during the programme. If you have a mini, medium or large laptop; so far it works fine, you are good to go.

Who can apply?

Ladies between the ages of 18 - 35 who want to gain knowledge of the technology world or start a career in wed development can apply for the programme.

Are there internship opprotunities?

Yes, there are internship opportunities for scholars who meet up with the minimum 95% attendance requirement and do well in their group and personal projects.

Will I be certified?

Yes, you will be certified on successful completion of the programme.

How long does the programme run?

The programme runs for 8-9 weeks every saturdays.
